The measuring hopper installation location must be on a mount parts located in a large vertical shaft-type niches, serious damage of shaft lining structure, especially the loading area in shaft wall rock with poor locations, support difficult. In this context it has been studied to a level measurement of fix quantifies loading equipment, narrow shaft recess size, to solve these problems.
