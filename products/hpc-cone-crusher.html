HPC cone crusher mainly consists of main parts, eccentric shaft sleeve, main shaft assembly, support sleeve assembly, moving cone assembly, fixed cone assembly etc. The motor drives eccentric sleeve through v-belt, transmission shaft, bevel gear. The moving cone wings with the force of eccentric shaft sleeve so that the crushing surface is close to the mortar from time to time. In this way, the materials will be crushed through continuous pressing, and the crushed materials will be discharged the discharge opening.
