Before long distance delivery of mobile crusher, dismantle fixtures, then load and transmit separately. Those left should get fixed in case of shaking, impacting and waving during delivery at a high speed. Check the mobile crushing plant carefully before transmit, especially pulling parts and skid device, to avoid accident. When everything is prepared, remember to unload the landing legs. When our mobile rock crusher used in job location needs to move a small distance, just check the travelling gear and unload the landing legs. Then move the mobile crusher with high traction force machines, such as bulldozer. During delivery, do not move too quickly. For moving in job location, pay attention to pavement behavior, especial keep a cross slope.
