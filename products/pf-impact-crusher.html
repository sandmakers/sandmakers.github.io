PF Impact Crusher

SBM's PF Impact Crusher is widely used as secondary crusher in stone processing line. Our PF Impact Crusher has a number of features. It absorbs the world's most advanced manufacturing processes. Perfect products and considerate service make SMB highly recognized by our customers.

  • Simple structure and easy maintenance

    Machine structure is simple, mainly consists of casing, the rotor and counter plates. You can replace parts and maintain through the device on the machine's top.

  • Energy saving and long service time

    The machine is with high crushing ratio, high efficiency and low energy consumption. The hammer is made of high chrome, impact resistance and abrasion resistance, effectively extending the service life of wearing parts.

  • Wide applications

    This crusher can process materials whose size below 500mm and compression strength less than 360Mpa. Therefore, PF Jaw Crusher is widely used in many industries, such as chemical, metallurgy, road and bridge construction and sand making and so on.

  • Final particle size adjustable and in good shape

    The machine with the discharge port adjusting device which can control the size of final material. Finished products with cubic shape, no tension cracks, can meet all kinds of sand and gravel aggregate grading standards.

Impact crusher crushing materials with impact force. When materials enter, crushed due to the high-speed impact of the blow bar and are thrown to the impact plates on the rotor for secondary crush. Then materials will be shot back to the blow bar again for the third crush. Process repeats until the materials are crushed to the required size and discharged from the lower part of the machine. Size and shape of the finished powder can be changed by adjusting the gap between the impact rack and rotor support. Impact crusher employs self-weigh security device in its back frame. When other objects enter the impact cavity, they will be forced out of the machine by the impact rack in the front and back of the machine.
