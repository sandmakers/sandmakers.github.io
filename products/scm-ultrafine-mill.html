SCM Ultrafine Mill

By absorbing foreign advanced technology, SBM designed SCM Ultrafine Mill. SCM Ultrafine Mill is an efficient ultrafine mill which is widely used in limestone processing line. This grinding mill can meet customers' requirement of thin powder and super thin powder. This great mill and our considerable service will make our customers feel satisfied.

  • High fineness and flexible adjustment

    The fineness can be arbitrarily adjusted between 325 ~ 2500 mesh, product fineness can reach D97 ≤ 5μm once time.

  • Clean and environmentally friendly

    Adopted deduster and muffler, in order to decrease pollution and noise and reach the requirement of national environmental protection.

  • Low production cost, high-performance

    Under the same circumstances of finished product fineness and motor power, SCM Ultrafine Mill's production increases 40%, while its energy consumption is only one-third of the jet mill.

The main motor drives main axle and every layer rotating by the power of reducer. Dial drives numbers of rollers rolling and rotating in the ring by pintles. Big materials are crushed into small particles by hammer crusher. Then they are sent to store house by elevator. Vibrating feeder send the materials evenly to the middle of the upper dial. Under the function of the eccentricity, the materials fall into the ring to be pressed, crushed and milled into pieces by rollers. After the first crush, materials fall into the second and third layer. The pumping of the high-pressure centrifugal blower put the outside air into the machine. The coarse powder mill will be brought into the classifier.
