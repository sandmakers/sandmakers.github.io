To adjust the height of the gate and to control of the liquid level are the useful ways for foam scraper by being scraped off. The slurry with the pharmaceutical treatment can be fixed in bubbles above with some mineral particles selectively, which is scraped off by floating on the surface, and the rest can be still retained in the slurry in order to reach the purpose of sorting the material.

The products involved in the field of mineral processing equipment, in particular applied to mineral processing equipment of automatic feeding control system.
 
Currently, when processing equipment production, usually artificial feeding. Because human can see scrap parts of the equipment, for example, ball mill grinding tube, sometimes feeding less often occurs, and devices cannot give full play to efficiency; charging too much at times and could easily lead to overloading of equipment, affecting the service life of the equipment, or even cause equipment damage, especially when operating at night, due to the workers ' cause, sometimes causing individual devices idling.
 
This product applies the technical problem to be solved is to provide automatic feeding control system of mineral processing equipment, able to realize mineral processing equipment of feeding automation, reducing production costs.
 
In order to solve the technical problems mentioned above, this product is suitable for automatic feeding control system of mineral processing equipment, including Ml of main motor and transmission motor M2, the automatic control system also includes connected with the main motor Ml Ml-current ammeter is used to display the main motor of Al and Ml are used to control the main motor operation of AC contactor KM1, Through hot following electrical RJ and conveying motor M2 connection of exchange contact device KM2 and according to ammeter Al of information for control Exchange contact device KMl and exchange contact device KM2 run of control module 4; by said control module 4 including overload alarm module 3, respectively and overload alarm module 3 connection of middle following electrical KAl and middle following electrical KA2, respectively receives from overload alarm module 3 output signal of middle following electrical ZJl and middle following electrical ZJ2; Intermediate relay ZJl receives signals transmitted to AC contactor KM1, ZJ2 receives signals transmitted to the intermediate relay AC contactor KM2.
