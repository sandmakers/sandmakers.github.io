  • Good stability and high reliability

    The electric control system adopts centralized controlled, so the mill is with high degree of automation. As a result there is no need of people in the operating room.

    The materials stay in the mill with short residence time, so it is easy to detect and control the product particle size and chemical composition. Ensuring the quality of the final product by reducing duplication of milling.

The motor drives the grinding table through decelerator. The materials fall down the center of grinding table from feed opening. At the same time, hot air comes into the mill from the air inlet. Due to the centrifugal force, materials move to the edge of the grinding table. The materials are pulverized by the roller when passing through the groove on the grinding table. The crushed materials are brought up by vane high-speed airstream, the larger particles fall down to the grinding table for regrinding. When the materials in the airstream pass the separator on the top of the mill, the coarse powder fall down the grinding table for regrinding under the function of rotation rotor. The fine powder comes out with the airstream, and is gathered by the dust catcher. The materials content with moisture will be dried when they meet the hot airstream. Through adjusting the temperature of the hot airstream, it can meet different material’s requirement, and also through adjusting separator, it can reach proper fineness of materials.
