MTM Trapezium Grinder

According to many years' mill manufacture experience, SBM designed large capacity MTM Trapezium Grinder, which has been widely used in milling process. Compared with other kind of mills, it has incomparable advantages. If you are seeking for a grinding mill with perfect performance, SBM's MTM Trapezium Grinder is your best choice.

  • Low production cost, high-performance

    The design of rollers and ring in the main machine adopts an inverted trapezium, which can control the slip speed of material between rollers and rings. As a result, it can increase the grinding time greatly, and enhance the crushing effect.

  • Good stability, and high reliability

    The powder classifier adopts high density and precise impeller device, which can increase precision and capacity. And the mill equipped with efficient centrifugal induced draft fan to improves efficiency.

  • Good products size composition

    The trapezium grinder adopts convenient impeller adjustment device, so users can adjust the size of the final product according to their requirements.

In the grinding mill, air is driven into the undersurface of the grinding ring and flows upward, carrying fines to the classifying section. The classifier allowed the pulverized material to pass and the oversized particles are drove back to the grinding chamber for further processing. The whole mill operates under negative pressure conditions, which can maximize the service life of span>major mechanics components. Thus minimizing mill maintenance and plant housekeeping problems have been solved.
