Motor feeder structure and its use can be divided into three types: gzd type feeder, vibrating feeder zsw type feeder gzz type.
Vibration feeder works:
Gzd type feeder consists of vibrating frame idea, vibration electric motor, spring, supporting seat and wear-resistant linings and other components.
Because vibration electric motor starts, so that the rack on a support spring for forced vibration, the material is this vibration as a driving force for sliding and throwing motion in the trough, so that the material feed forward and achieve objectives.
