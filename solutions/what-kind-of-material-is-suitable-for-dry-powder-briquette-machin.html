What Kind Of Material Is Suitable For Dry Powder Briquette Machin What Kind Of Material Is Suitable For Dry Powder Briquette Machin

During the processing of dry powder briquette machine, the choice of material is a very important part, because the dry powder briquette machine is a kind of non water, non additive and direct pressing equipment. therefore, the materials of dry powder briquette machine should have certain humidity, and which can form and keep stable shape without strong interaction resulted from adding additives.
First of all, it should be ensured that dry powder briquette machine is different from other briquetting machines, which is more stringent on the requirements of materials, for example, the material should not have impurities. even though many other briquetting machines are requested not to have impurities, the emphasis is not the same, and they mean that impurities are hard material blocks which are relatively large. however, the dry powder briquette machine is much more stringent, because it has no addition of binder, so the materials must have very high purity. in order to press the materials, the dry powder briquetting machine must have blocks or high hardness ores inside it, so as not to damage its roller or the bearing. when use the dry powder briquette machine, it is better to combine with the screening equipment in the process recipe to avoid iron ore.
