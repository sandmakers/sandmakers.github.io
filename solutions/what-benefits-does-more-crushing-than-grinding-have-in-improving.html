What Benefits Does More Crushing Than Grinding Have in Improving What Benefits Does More Crushing Than Grinding Have in Improving

Ore grinding is an crucial step in ore dressing process, aimed at grinding the ores into smaller particles so as to facilitate the next-step operations of magnetic separation and flotation. in the grinding process, ball mill is a piece of frequently used equipment for ore grinding.
In ore Portable Crusher grinding operation:
when ball mill is grinding ores, both the feeding particle size and diameter of the small ores have an significant influence on its grinding efficiency. generally speaking, when grinding, the ores must go Cone Crusher through the coarse crushing, secondary crushing and fine crushing before entering into ball mill. because, in theory, the principle of “more crushing than grinding” is a kind of effective way for energy conservation and environmental protection. in the meantime, it can promote the grinding efficiency of ball mill, because ball mill is a type of high-energy-consuming equipment. and under the condition of high crushing ratio, ball mill has a better crushing effect, contributing to a higher grinding efficiency of ball mill.
in mining industry, there is an old saying goes that more crushing and less grinding. that is to say, the crushing process is more economical and convenient compared with the grinding process, and that theformer has a higher efficiency. if the large-block materials can be crushed small enough and uniform enough before entering into the grinding equipment, it will greatly reduce the load of the grinding equipment and thereby directly improve the production capacity of the milling machine. besides, after the materials have been broken into enough-small pieces, during transportation and storage, the separation phenomena between materials of different particle sizes will be significantly reduced, very beneficial to improving the accuracy of burdening.
