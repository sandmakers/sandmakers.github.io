What Fields Can Dry Modularization Sand Making System Be Used What Fields Can Dry Modularization Sand Making System Be Used

Dry modularization sand making system is a new type of complete set of sand making equipment independently researched and developed by henan SBM mining machinery co., ltd. this system adopts overlapping Raymond Mill structure, and the main engine part adopts german original rotary centrifugal sand maker. according to the customer specific needs, the main engine part can also adopt domestic impact type sand maker. as this sand making system is widely used, then what fields can it be used?
(1) crushing and recycling of casting waste sand.
The disposal of casting waste sand is always a headache for every casting foundry, and this is no exception for henan SBM mining machinery co., ltd., which is integrated with the research and development and production of crushing equipment and wear-resisting accessories. dry modularization sand making system is a new product specially designed for crushing and recycling casting waste sand. in addition, our company is dealing with casting waste sand with this sand making system, using vertical shaft impact crusher as the main engine. this sand making system has been put into use and it can save 800,000~900,000 rmb of quartz sand acquisition expenses every year.
(2) Mobile Crusher crushing of construction waste
Dry modularization sand making system is also suitable to be used in the construction waste crushing and brick making production line and has the advantages of small coverage area, low investment, modularization assemble, strong flexibility and high economic benefits. in addition, this system belongs to semi-mobile sand making production line, which has lower production cost than mobile crushing station and is more convenient than the fixed construction waste disposal production line.
(3) sand making in stone plant and concrete mixing station
In fact, dry modularization sand making system was originally designed for stone plant and concrete mixing station to process the 5~100mm stone materials into high-quality artificial sand, thus turning the waste into treasure and improving the benefits of stone plant and concrete mixing station. especially the dry type sand making system with german original sand maker can achieve high efficiency and low investment.
